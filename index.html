To dear Joyce

发表于 2018-12-28

  In September 2018,I met a special girl. Her name is 蒲艺.Fate brings us together.In my eyes,she is a kindhearted and brave girl who always face life with smile,face trouble with iron.And she is sweet.she is one with strong self-esteem…All these glitters is gold to me.Baby I just wanna say that you’re unique and one of a kind,and your appearance is the best gift to me in 2018.I believe that we can go anywhere we want,we can do anything if we put our minds to it.So just take my hand and come with me to start a brand new and colorful life. I promise you that I will cherish our friendship and love you,I will trust you and honor you,I will laugh with you and cry with you,Through the best and the worst,Through the difficult and the easy.What may come I will always be there.

What's past is prologue

发表于 2018-12-28

  Blink 2018 is about to end.Feeling like I traveled through some time and skipping through some years,but it feels right. There’s been moments I’m really proud of. And a lot of moments I look back and I’m pretty disappointed in myself for,but the things that I’ve done really don’t define who I am. I’m a confident and proud person who always have a desire to win … to excel.And through it all I lost some of my best qualities. For that I’m sorry.But what I can say is I’m looking forward to being someone that you guys can all look at and be proud of…someone you can smile at and see some of yourself in.Someone close to me once said,”It’s how you rise from a fall that truly defines you as a man.”I used to want to be anybody but myself.But after seen many people next to me still choose to trust me and get my back no matter what happened,I decide to be myself from now on.Because I dont wanna let them down,let myself down.I’m lucky enough to have experienced so many things which make me grow as a person.
Though there are still many challenges waiting for me in the future.But I’m excited for these challenges and I want to say thank you so much for taking this journey with me,and I’m excited for you to see what’s next.Thank you,God,for your grace and for never giving up on me.

  So here is something I want to say to me for the 2019.For what it’s worth,it’s never too late.To be whoever you
want to be.There’s no limit.Start whenever you want.You can change the or stay the same.And there’s no rules to this thing.people can make the best or the worst of it.And I hope you make the best of it.I hope you see things that startle you.I hope you feel things you never felt before.I hope you meet people with different point of view.I hope you live a life you’re proud of.And if you find that you’re not.I hope you have the strength to start all over again…to color your life with the chaos of troubles.Dig deeper,work harder,got better and become stronger for the people who always supporting and loving you and for yourself.You can’t undo the past.All you can do is face what’s ahead.


Cody Hsiang

近期的一些学习及生活感想

发表于 2018-11-05 
1.英语真的非常重要，一个男性要是英语好，那么他的机会将会多太多。

2.平时的课程并不是每一门都是有意义的，需要自己选择到底是白白让
身体困在教室两节课，还是利用课堂时间做一些自己想做又有意义的事
情。虽然听起来可能有些不尊重老师什么的，但是我觉得大学听课与否
选择权在自己手里，大学是自由的，而且大部分老师也很开明并不会强
迫你去听课。因此没必要有心理负担。
                Just follow your heart.

3.大学你学不学，没人管你。必须要培养自己的自学能力，和自控
能力。课余时间很充裕也很自由，完全可以利用起来培养爱好兴趣
 开发新技能和提升自己。

4.鱼和熊掌不可兼得，要理性做出选择取舍。每个人追求的东西不同，
明确自己的目标，绝对不能受到他人的影响。

5.“只有共同利用价值处于同一水平线，你们才会成为朋友。”这是我不
久前在知乎上看到的一句话，感同身受。同与自己志同道合的人相处很
美妙也很轻松。学会感恩朋友圈，学会欣赏和发现朋友圈里的人的闪光
之处---身边的人优秀，你将接触到更多优秀的人；反过来你自己卓越
了，能聚拢到的人必然也是卓越的。

6.团队精神>>个人主义
